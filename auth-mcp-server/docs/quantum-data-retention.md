# Quantum Dynamics Data Lifecycle Policy

## Scope
This policy governs data handling for Quantum Dynamics' ProAnalytics platform and all customer research data managed by our DataLabs division.

## Retention Schedules
**Customer Project Data**: 7 years after project completion or contract termination
**Research Analytics**: Stored in LabVault system for 5 years, then archived to ColdStore facility
**Financial Records**: 7 years in ActiveBooks system, quarterly exports to ComplianceArchive
**Employee Records**: Current + 3 years in HRConnect database
**System Logs**: 18 months in LogTracker, then purged automatically

## Data Classification
- **Level 1 (Public)**: Marketing materials, published research (indefinite retention)
- **Level 2 (Internal)**: Employee communications, planning docs (3 years)
- **Level 3 (Confidential)**: Customer data, financial records (7 years)
- **Level 4 (Restricted)**: Trade secrets, proprietary algorithms (permanent retention)

## Disposal Procedures
Electronic data disposal managed through SecureWipe Pro using DOD 5220.22-M standard. Physical media destruction performed by CertDestroy Inc. with certificates maintained in the ComplianceVault system.

## Legal Hold
When litigation is anticipated, all relevant data is preserved using the HoldFast system. Legal department triggers holds via the LegalOps portal, affecting AutoPurge processes until release.
